Software
PlantBuddies uses the open source software Piwik for web analytics.

Cookies & anonymity
Piwik uses cookies to help us to understand how users use the site. In this case cookies are text files that are stored on you computer and make your browser identifiable.
We will never use this data to identify you as a person.

Storage
The information about your use of the website (including your IP address anonymized prior to its storage) will be stored on the server of our service provider in Germany.

What for?
We will only use this data to improve the experience on PlantBuddies and not for commercial or marketing reasons.
